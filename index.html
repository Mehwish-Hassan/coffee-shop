What makes Our Coffee so Special

Specialty coffee is exclusively made of a family of varieties under the Arabica species. Coffea Arabica is a delicate plant that is prone to diseases and requires specific conditions to grow- rich soil and high altitudes.

Some other factors that make a difference are pesticide use, organic growing practices, and batch size.
